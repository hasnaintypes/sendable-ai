# AI Email Generation

Sendable uses AI to generate personalized cold emails tailored to each recipient.

## How It Works

1. **Input context** — Provide information about your target recipient or company.
2. **AI generates** — The system crafts a personalized email using the provided context.
3. **Review and send** — Edit the generated email if needed, then send it directly.

## Features

### Personalization

Each email is generated with context-aware personalization. The AI considers:

- Recipient's role and company
- Industry-specific language
- Your outreach goals (sales, recruiting, networking)

### Templates

Sendable includes responsive email templates built with [React Email](https://react.email).
Templates are rendered server-side and support:

- Custom branding and styling
- Dynamic content injection
- Mobile-responsive layouts

## Usage

From the dashboard, navigate to the email composer to start generating emails.
The AI will suggest content based on the context you provide.
